üìñ Welcome & How to Use This Planner

Welcome! This planner helps you and your partner document all the essential household information you need. Work through it together at your own pace over the coming months - there's no rush at all.

What's Inside

This planner has six modules covering everything you need to know:

  1. Financial Overview - Bank accounts, savings, investments, pensions
  2. Monthly Money Management - Bills, utilities, insurance, subscriptions
  3. Property & Utilities - Home systems, heating, water, electrical
  4. Home Maintenance Calendar - Regular tasks and trusted tradespeople
  5. Important Documents & Digital Access - Legal papers, wills, passwords
  6. Healthcare & Essential Contacts - Medical info and your support network

How to Work Through This

  • Click the modules above: Use the navigation menu to jump between sections
  • Take your time: 30-45 minute sessions work well - one module every couple of weeks is perfect
  • Work together: This is about both of you knowing what's what
  • Print it out: Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac) anytime to print - keep a paper copy somewhere safe
  • Search easily: Press Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F) to find anything quickly - try searching "boiler" or "pension"
  • Come back anytime: Update information when things change

üîí Security Note: This planner will contain sensitive information. Don't write actual passwords or PINs in here - just note where they're kept (like "password book in desk drawer"). Keep this document secure and tell trusted family or your solicitor where to find it.
